Data & Privacy

What data is collected?

  • Page content for AI analysis (products, prices, reviews)
  • Your API key (stored locally, encrypted)
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Session data for cross-tab functionality

How is your data protected?

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • API keys are encrypted before storage
  • No data is sent to third-party servers except OpenAI
  • Session data is automatically cleaned after 24 hours
